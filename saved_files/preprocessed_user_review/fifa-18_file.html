It's such pandering to the US market it's sickening. In FIFA 17 I started with a small club thinking I would be able to get transferred to any big club in the world, but nope it was completely scripted, so I was stuck at Watford. This time I wouldn't get burned so as an AMC I signed for Manchester United. Real Madrid is a great club, but I don't care for them irl, so it felt awkward being forced to leave United, and I was glad the deal fell through. The game goes on and the scripted event starts being 3-0 down. My difficulty settings were set way too low and I scored 6 times after coming on. This has been so frustrating for years, give me the gameplay of pes in the fifa polish and we would have the perfect football game. I got an email to inform me of winning the championship... This isn't right.

EA Sports, you have a great example in PES on how the game can flow, so please use it for a change.
