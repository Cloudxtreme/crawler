It    Fractured space arrives late to the already crowded MOBA scene, but it arrives having learned from the success of those who had gone before in the genre and avoids many of the pitfall that blighted those that fell. A tactical 5v5 capital ship space game. What's not to like with this level of polish.

    Upon release we are greeted with a game fit to take e-sports into the next decade. It comes replete with a vast array of ships, each unique and easy to pick up but difficult to truly master. At first the games main mode conquest strikes you as shallow and throw away but after a few games it opens up to reveal itself. It is one of those rare things in the MOBA field a game that at its core is immensely deep, tactical and values skill and team work over all else. It also destroys the notion of kill stealing as any one who does 20% damage to an enemy is awarded a take down or kill. Graphically stunning in HD with Smooth controls to boot. It gives the sensation of captaining a Battlestar Galactica size ship for free, Yes free so there is no excuse not to try it.
