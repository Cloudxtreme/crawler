    Pros:
    -World is big
    -Hero is basically superman and allows for a lot of mindless shooting/fighting
    -Some interesting bases for the series were thrown here like the grappling hook(only works on cars) and the parachute

    That's it really.

    -No memorable music

    None of this is that bad though, because Just Cause as a series is all about the fun that the gameplay provides.

    And uuuuuugh.

Control in cars and vehicles is however ludicrous. It works, sure, but the entire physics engine is just the most basic thing in the game: if you hit a rock at 140km/h, you'll go to 0 in 0 seconds.     Parachute also sucks to control, so there's no fun in anything that's not you on foot.

    The game is usually about 3 things:
    -Driving around
    -Shooting people
    -Shooting people in a battle

All you have is a huge map with lots of copypasted villages and cities and some missions that are usually about shooting more stuff, and you go from settlement to settlement, mission to mission, driving with poor mechanics, then shooting some enemies, shooting them in a town, shooting them in a field, shooting them near a mansion...
