    Though the shooting and combat here feels awful no matter which weapon you use…and the aiming is awkward
    This game is 2 hours long and takes place inside of a school as you go through looking for the right route.. Keeping your intoxication level just right so you don’t die... and killing all of the zombies in your way...
    That’s it... this is the game…
    The level design isn’t really interesting…
