The clever use of ground harness rather than arbitrary biomes is admirable, while the cloning of extinct animals is done in a much cleverer way than any other game. The attention to detail is astonishing, with resources coming in and rubbish going out. Another way this game scores above the others is the realistic behaviour of guests, who do not freak out when a peafowl is not in its enclosure. The gameplay is smart, with a range of missions that are not simply "build a zoo", and instead can range from chasing Yetis around the Himalayas, to creating an ecosystem with Horses, Wolves, Tigers and Snow hares.
