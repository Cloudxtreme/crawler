    Then wait for people to get separated, stab them - bug out though because you don't want to be seen with a fresh corpse, that's how you get busted.
    Rinse and repeat, and trust me - this will take quite some time to get all people dead.

    Now, the issue with this is:
    The NPC behavior is randomized, just like what is available in certain stages. For instance, sometimes you get a bomb, sometimes you get spare clothes. Sometimes there's some speaker that blows up, sometimes not.

    - stealth
    - cascade cause and effect (panic)
    - traps and situational benefits

    now - stealth should be the obvious part of the game ... your job is to murder people without getting the suspicion of others - or if it happens ... to get away. It works well enough - but could be better indeed. I ran into just a little too many situations where i was utterly puzzled that i got away with (like murdering someone literally next to someone else ... who was looking at me all the time ... but not reacting whatsoever).

    The big element of the game should be the cascade effects. Getting a body spotted - and the ensuing panic of the remaining people. But that is where the game fails - HARD. There certainly is an effect - but all in all.... people calm down just too much, too quickly and return to normal behaviour. Yes - there is a panic meter ... but it does not fill up nearly enough people quickly enough.

    The lack of a more realistic cascade effect makes that game too easy in my opinion. - On that part - i was surprised ... because i found that game to be EASY - maybe a bit too easy (doesn t mean i got every level on the first attempt - but it means i never needed more than 2 or 3 attempts) - many of the reviews i had read stated that the game was hard. (easy refers to solo, non twitch play)

    I would rather have enjoyed much harder victims - but better traps and items (and more) - For a game like that i would have loved to see some Rube-Goldberg traps - along with more innovative items.

    Lastly there is the idea of exploiting a situation with traps and behaviour. That means - attracting or repelling victims to get as many into a deadly trap as possible. The system mechanics work - there is a whole lot of situational fun to get for catching a bunch of people in a trap.

    The game changes tremendously when Twitch is taking an influence - it can become a lot harder indeed.

    I can recommend that game - but one must be aware that it is a very short game indeed with few stages that are more designed for the casual gamer. A more dedicated gamer breezes through the game in no time.

    I am not sure about extra content, mods or even an editor in the future ... an editor and player made levels might give that game a long, long life ... but in its vanilla state - its fun - but very short lived fun only.    … Expand
