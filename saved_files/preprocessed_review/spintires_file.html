    No reviews really show off just how good this games actually is. One of the best    I have been following Spin Tires development since 2011. I supported the game on kick starter for $150, got access to the early private beta and I have been playing this a ton! I have 44 hours logged upon writing this review and I highly recommend this game to honestly just about anyone who owns a PC...

    No reviews really show off just how good this games actually is. One of the best features of the game is soft body physics something I have not seen in any other game. Tree branches flex and bend around your truck as you drive through the woods, the truck chassis cab and all its pieces bend and flex as you drive over uneven terrain, the rubber tires actually deform over the ground, mud tracks are physically made into the terrain in real time meaning if you circle around and drive over them your tires will bounce over the track you just made! All mud tracks are saved so if you drive over a road continuously it will get harder and harder to get through. If you crash your truck into the mud, the mud will stay on your truck exactly where it hit into the ground, drive a clean wheel over a small patch of mud and only that area will get covered in mud, chunks of mud will cling to your wheels and vehicle and slowly fall off... seriously the amount of detail the developers have achieved in this game has set a new standard in the PC gaming industry.

    The water in this game will blow your mind. Rivers, swamps, flooded roads, ponds, all look completely different some are muddy murky water others are clear and blue, the wide range of water is like no other game out there, its not just a water "plane" pasted across the entire map. The physics of the water actually deform flow and move around objects including your truck. Drive through a shallow pond and your wheels actually push the water around, drive through deep water and the textures on your truck turn wet only in the area the water hits. Dare to take a small jeep into a river and you will get carried down stream as your engine gets damaged and stalls!

    Some of the other features of this game is the ability to use a winch. You can winch onto a nearby tree and pull yourself out of the mud, use your winch to tow a vehicle back to your garage for repairs, or use your winch to help a friend pull themselves out of the mud while in 4 player online multi-player! A fully functional hydraulic crane can be installed on trucks to pick up logs, rocks, help roll over vehicles etc. The hydraulics actually have physics, a fully extended crane is much weaker at picking items up then if it were by the trucks side. All of the vehicles have an attachment system so you can mix and match the trucks abilities to your liking, such as a trailer hitch to pull large trailers, utility to repair damaged vehicles, cistern to refuel vehicles, garage points used to unlock new garages on the map, several different log carrying attachments and trailers and more!

    Many people say this Is not really a "game" but they are just wrong. Spin Tires has plenty of objectives that can be accomplished in various ways. The main goal of the game is to delivery logs from a lumber yard to each of the objectives on each of the 5 maps. Secondary goals are to unlock new garages which you can use to refuel and repair, find unlock and rescue abandoned vehicles scattered across the maps, unlock cloaking points that hide the map. You can do all of this single player or in small 4 player online matches. There are even over 40 steam achievements to unlock!

    Mods will make this game absolutely insane. The demo from last year has over 700 mods you could use, maps and trucks. Once they add mod support in this in a future update this game will go through the roof.

    This game has a big learning curve but once you learn how to play it can be a lot of fun. If this review sounds interesting at all please buy this game it is very much so worth $30!
