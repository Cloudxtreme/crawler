    The gameplay is fairly straight-forward, it's a first person nod to the indiana jones in most adventurous types (you can tell when one of the characters - the main one surprisingly- says something "belongs in a museum" - if that's not a nod to Indy Jones then I don't know what is).
    The plot is reasonable enough, it's not a bad attempt - I am confused by the lack of swastikas though, which would have added to the general appearance of the game, although I am aware that there are countries with certain laws prohibiting the use of swastikas the way that the Third Reich used them (which coincidentally, was the wrong way round) - so never mind on that one.
    The one thing that did strike me a bit of an oddity more than a complaint really - as I know what it's like to have your name mispronounced...a lot - and that is during the first few cutscenes there are at least three different ways of saying Quatermain. kwatamain, kwartermain, etc. Listen for the differences, it's kind of a side note more than a main issue but it kinda adds to the quirky side of the game instead of ruining it outright.
    This has had a surprising number of positive reviews, but only 6.9 on the user score which is kinda disappointing, it's a good game despite it's not too shabby but not great graphics, and it's fairly simple approach to being a shooter game.
