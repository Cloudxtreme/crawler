Summary: With its 2013 edition, Pro Cycling Manager pushes race realism with a more refined Career mode. Use its remodelled and improved interface to better prepare for your season. Negotiate the team’s objectives with your sponsors, and plan the races/your riders’ objectives according to the desired results and each rider’s expectations. Strive to satisfy each member of the team throughout the season with regard to their race schedule: take into account the prestige of each race, and ensure that the event matches the rider’s profile otherwise it may knock his satisfaction.
