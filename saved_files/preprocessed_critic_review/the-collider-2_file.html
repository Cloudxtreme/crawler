    The Collider 2 is a fast-paced arcade game where you have to launch yourself in a suicide mission, piloting a fleet of Starfighters to destroy an alien mothership from the inside, avoiding collisions, collecting artifacts and destroying key targets. The game is easy to pick up but very challenging to master. It features a mission mode with 54 levels and 6 airships to unlock, and a survival mode with online ladders to compete with friends and players from around the world. Oculus Rift and HTC Vive VR headsets are also supported. The game offers intense and thrilling action. Racing arcade fans and players in search for hard challenges will love it.
